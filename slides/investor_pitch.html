Defensibility

Why Not ChatGPT?

General LLMs fail at finance for three structural reasons. Vinsight is purpose-built to solve each one.

1

Sycophancy → Adversarial Debate

Ask ChatGPT "Why is TSLA a good buy?" and it agrees. Our architecture forces a Bear Agent to attack your thesis. The structure prevents bias, not the prompt.

2

Hallucination → Deterministic Math

Our scoring engine is Python-computed. The AI provides narrative only — score authority is locked to deterministic code with ±10pt bounded adjustment.

3

Generic NLP → Domain FinBERT

92% accuracy on financial text vs 65% for generic models. Understands that "increased debt" is negative and "growth" in context of revenue is positive.

Three Compounding Moats

Behavioral Intelligence (user psychology dataset) · Agent Workflow Network Effects (shared templates) · Fact-Grounded Data (structured SEC intelligence)
